Labour Day




The origin of Labour day is from United States because in 19th Century there was a lot of industrialization and the workers have to do work for 15 hours a day and after the Labour Union Movement there was right that all the workers will do 8 hours work and 8 hours rest and they will also get paid leaves. In India first time Labour day was celebrated in Chennai by the Labour Kisan Party of Hindustan on 1/05/1923 ,also red flag which symbolises Labour Day was used in India first and their leader Malayapuram Singaravelu Chettiar stated that the government should announce a national holiday on Labour Day in India and since then the country has continued to celebrate. In 2016 on Labour Day world saw a lot of marches , protest going on like places such as Istanbul, Moscow and Taipei.



Interesting Fact

In Hindi Labour Day is called as Kamgar Din or Antarrashtriya Shramik Diwas,in Marathi it is called Kamgar Divas and in Tamil it is called Uzhaipalar Naal.
